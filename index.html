Game Instructions

Objective

Control the animal (dog or rabbit) and help it collect the right food while avoiding harmful ones.

Controls

Use the arrow keys to move the animal.

Scoring

  • Dog: Earns points by collecting meat or candy.
  • Rabbit: Earns points by collecting carrots or candy.

Lives

  • Dog: Loses a life when touching a carrot or bug.
  • Rabbit: Loses a life when touching meat or a bug.
